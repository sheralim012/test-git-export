Density matrices review material: overview

This section includes review material from Prof. Peter Shor's 8.370 lectures on

  • what noisy quantum states are,

  • the indistinguishability of density matrix ensemble unravelings,

  • measurements and density matrices,

  • the action of unitary transforms on density matrices,

  • the effect of measurement on an ensemble of states,

  • density matrix properties,

  • density matrices and the partial trace, and

  • the Bloch sphere representation of single qubit density matrices.

Also available are two of Prof. Isaac Chuang's tablet videos from 8.371, about

  • quantum mechanics and quantum circuits, and

  • unravelings of density matrices.

Additional review material about quantum states and quantum measurement can be found in the 8.370.1x online course, and in 8.05x.
